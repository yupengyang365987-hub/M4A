        Tips for Better Scripts

        Opening Statements

        • Start with a compelling hook
        • Outline your theory of the case
        • Preview key evidence
        • End with a clear roadmap

        Cross Examination

        • Use leading questions
        • Control the witness
        • Build to your points systematically
        • Keep questions short and clear

        Closing Arguments

        • Review the evidence presented
        • Connect evidence to your theory
        • Address opposing arguments
        • End with a powerful call to action
