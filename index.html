Our Mission

Mock For All (M4A) is dedicated to democratizing access to high-quality mock trial education. We believe every student deserves the tools and guidance to excel in advocacy, regardless of their school's resources.

Through AI-powered feedback, expert video tutorials, and a vibrant community, we're building the future of mock trial education—one student at a time.
